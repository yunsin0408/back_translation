# Light Replacement Procedure

## Introduction

This document details the procedure for replacing the front and rear lights on a bicycle, specifically involving the replacement of yellow bulbs with white bulbs and modification of the glass cover.

## Safety Precautions

*   Refer to warning and caution references: `c001`, `c002`, `w001` for detailed safety information.

## Preliminary Requirements

### Locations

*   `wla-0001`: Front handlebar location.
*   `wla-0002`: Rear saddle location.

### Tools & Materials

*   **Toolset:** Special toolset containing a screwdriver (reference: `seq-0001`).
*   **Screwdriver:**  Special screwdriver (reference: `seq-0002`) from the toolset.
*   **Front Bulb (New):** White bulb (reference: `spa-0003`).
*   **Rear Bulb (New):** White bulb (reference: `spa-0004`).
*   **Glass Cover:** Glass (reference: `spa-0005`).
*   **Modified Glass Cover:** Glass with vent hole (reference: `spa-0007`).  Original glass (reference: `spa-0008`) can be modified.
*   **Bulb Kit:** Kit containing new bulbs (reference: `spa-0002`).
*   **Discarded Bulb:** Yellow bulb (reference: `spa-0001`).

## Procedure

### 1. Front Light Replacement

1.  From location `wla-0001`, remove the glass cover (`spa-0005`).
2.  Remove the used yellow front bulb (`spa-0001`).
3.  Discard the used bulb (`spa-0001`).
4.  Remove the new white bulb (`spa-0003`) from the kit (`spa-0002`).
5.  Install the new white bulb (`spa-0003`).
6.  Install the glass cover (`spa-0005`) on the light.
7.  Attach the light fitting to the handlebar.
8.  Apply the protective strip around the handlebar.
9.  Install the washer on the screw.
10. Use the special screwdriver (`seq-0002`) from the toolset (`seq-0001`) and tighten the screw into the hole at the bottom of the clamp to secure it to the handlebar.

### 2. Rear Light Replacement

1.  From location `wla-0002`, remove the glass cover (`spa-0005`).
2.  Remove the used yellow rear bulb (`spa-0001`).
3.  Discard the used bulb (`spa-0001`).
4.  Remove the new white bulb (`spa-0004`) from the kit (`spa-0002`).
5.  Install the new white bulb (`spa-0004`).
6.  **Glass Modification (if applicable):** Drill a 4mm hole in the middle of the glass cover (`spa-0008`) to allow venting and heat evacuation. The modified glass cover is available as reference `spa-0007`.
7.  Install the modified (or unmodified) glass cover (`spa-0007` or `spa-0008`) on the light.

## Closing Requirements

*   Verify functionality by switching the lights on if necessary.
